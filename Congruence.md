This is a predicate

### Definition

Fix a positive natural number m.

For integers $a$ and $b$

>$a \cong b (mod (m))$ 
$\iff$ 
$m|(a-b)$
$\iff$
$a-b = m \cdot k$ for some integer k
$\iff$
$a = m \cdot k + b$ for some integer k

Note to [[Divisibility]]

### Examples
$18 \cong 2 (mod4)$
$2 \cong -2 (mod4)$
$18 \cong -2 (mod4)$
