[[Bijection]]

For a function $f: A \rightarrow B$, the following are equivalent.

1. $f$ is bijective
2. $\forall b \in B. !\exists a \in A. f(a) = b$
3. $(\forall b \in B. \exists a \in A. f(a) = b) \land (\forall a_1, a_2 \in A. f(a_1) = f(a_2) \Rightarrow a_1 = a_2)$
