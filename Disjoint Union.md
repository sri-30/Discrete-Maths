## Definition
The disjoint union $A \uplus B$ of two sets $A$ and $B$ is the set

$A \uplus B = (\set{1} \times A) \cup (\set{2} \times B)$

Thus,

$\forall x. x \in (A \uplus B) \iff (\exists a \in A. x = (1, a)) \lor (\exists b \in B. x = (2, b))$
