# [[Proposition 144]]
1. $\mathbb{N}, \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Q}$ are countable [[set]]s
2. The product and [[Disjoint Union]] of sountable sets is countable
3. Every [[finite]] set is countable
4. Every subset of a countable set is countable
