# Definition
Let $f: A \rightarrow B$ be a [[function]]. The direction image of $X \subseteq A$ under $f$ is the [[set]] $f(x) \subseteq B$, defined as
$\overrightarrow{f}(x) = \set{b \in B | \exists x \in X. f(x) = b} = \set{f(x) \in B| x \in X}$
